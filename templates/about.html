About

Explainable is all about demistifying the complex nature of Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning Algorithms by demonstrating how they make decisions in clear and readable formats. Our website allows for the uploading of images and tabular data against specific, pre-trained algorithms while our API and Python Plugins allow for users to have mroe direct control over their investigation.

Explainable was started in Fall of 2019 at Tufts University.

This is being completed for the Senior Capstone class.
